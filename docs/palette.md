# Xcode Dark-Inspired Palette

| Role | Hex | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Base background | `#292A2F` | Matches Xcode's canvas tone.
| Surface background | `#2B2B30` | Used for panels and popovers.
| Border / subtle lines | `#363A3C` | Divider strokes.
| Primary foreground | `#CED2D9` | Default text.
| Muted foreground | `#8E8E98` | Secondary text, inactive tabs.
| Accent blue | `#0A84FF` | macOS system blue for focus + links.
| Accent green | `#89C0B3` | Success state, string literal cues.
| Accent orange | `#FF9F0A` | Cursor + highlights.
| Accent pink | `#EE81B0` | Keywords, directives.
| Accent purple | `#AB83E4` | Types, class names.
| Accent cyan | `#64D2FF` | Functions, methods.
| Accent yellow | `#D6CA86` | Constants, decorators.
| Selection background | `#2D4F73` | Text + highlight selection.
| Line highlight | `#282830` | Active line background.
| Gutter background | `#1A1A1F` | Matches Xcode line number gutter.

These hues mirror macOS Big Sur+ UI accents, providing a faithful Xcode feel inside VS Code.
